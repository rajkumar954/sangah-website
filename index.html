Welcome to Sangah

Welcome to Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangah, a united community of earthmover owners, operators, and supporters committed to strength, unity, and service. Our Sangah represents the powerful spirit of collaboration where individuals from the earthmoving and construction industry come together to celebrate their work, support one another, and contribute to society. Through rallies, community events, welfare activities, and cultural initiatives, we aim to inspire pride in our profession while building a strong network rooted in respect, unity, and progress. Join us as we shape a future where teamwork, tradition, and social responsibility go hand in hand.

About Us

Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangah is a collective organisation formed to unite earthmover professionals, machinery operators, and community members under one banner of cooperation and mutual growth. Our Sangah actively works to promote unity among JCB and heavy machinery owners, conducts large-scale rallies and events, and participates in welfare initiatives that benefit society. We believe in uplifting our community through collaboration, skill-sharing, leadership development, and cultural participation. Guided by dedication, respect, and togetherness, Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangah strives to create a supportive environment where members help each other grow—professionally, socially, and culturally.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a vibrant, inclusive, and socially empowered community where people from all backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and support one another. We strive to create an environment where cultural heritage is preserved and celebrated, where every individual feels a strong sense of belonging, and where youth are inspired to lead with responsibility and compassion. By combining tradition with positive innovation, we aim to nurture a community that values unity, service, and collective well-being, ensuring that our cultural identity and spirit remain strong for generations to come.

Our Mission

The mission of Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangah is to bring earthmover owners, operators, and enthusiasts together through unity-driven initiatives, cultural programmes, and social welfare activities. We aim to encourage collaboration within the industry, promote professional growth, and organise large-scale rallies and events that showcase the strength and discipline of our Sangah. We are committed to supporting our members, uplifting society through service, and creating opportunities for learning, leadership, and participation. Our mission is to foster an atmosphere of trust, respect, and togetherness—ensuring that every member stands strong as part of one united Sangah.

101 Earthmovers Vehicle Tiranga Yatra

Venue: Gandhi Nagar, Indore

Date: 26th January 2026

Time:10:00 a.m. to 02:00 p.m.

Event Description The Grand JCB Rally is a historic and powerful celebration of unity, strength and national spirit. On 26th January 2026, a remarkable convoy of 101 JCB machines will travel together across the Super Corridor, creating a breathtaking visual tribute to the hardworking spirit that builds our nation. This event is not just a rally — it is a symbol of: Strength and progress, represented by the mighty fleet of JCBs Unity and coordination, showcased through the disciplined convoy movement Respect for the workforce that contributes daily to construction and development Patriotism, as the rally honors the significance of Republic Day Spectators will witness an extraordinary lineup of machinery, decorated in vibrant patriotic themes, moving in harmony as a mark of respect and pride. The rally aims to inspire the community, highlight the importance of infrastructure development, and bring people together in celebration of national achievements. Be part of this unforgettable moment where engineering meets patriotism, as 101 JCBs shape a grand vision of India’s strength and unity.
