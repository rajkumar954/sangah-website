Welcome to Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangh

Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangh is a united community of earthmover owners, operators, and supporters committed to strength, unity, and service. We bring together individuals from the earthmoving and construction industry to celebrate their work, support one another, and contribute to society through rallies, community events, welfare activities, and cultural programmes.

Join us as we build a future where teamwork, tradition, and social responsibility go hand in hand — supporting members professionally and uplifting our neighbourhoods.

About Us

Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangh is a collective organisation formed to unite earthmover professionals, machinery operators, and community members under one banner of cooperation and mutual growth. We promote unity among JCB and heavy machinery owners, organise large-scale rallies and events, and run welfare initiatives that benefit our neighbours.

Guided by dedication, respect and togetherness, our Sangh supports members to grow professionally, socially and culturally through training, collaboration and community service.

Our Vision

Our vision at Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangh is to build a vibrant, inclusive and socially empowered community where people from diverse backgrounds come together to learn, grow and support one another. We aim to preserve cultural heritage, foster belonging, and inspire the next generation to lead with responsibility and compassion.

Our Mission

The mission of Indore Earthmovers Ekta Sangh is to unite earthmover owners, operators and enthusiasts through unity-driven initiatives, cultural programmes and social welfare activities. We promote collaboration within the industry, encourage professional growth, and organise events that showcase the strength and discipline of our Sangh.

Recent Events

101 Earthmovers Vehicle Tiranga Yatra

Venue: Super Corridor, Indore

Date: 26 January 2026

Time: 10:00 AM – 02:00 PM

Event Description

The Grand JCB Rally is a historic celebration of unity, strength and national pride. On 26 January 2026, a convoy of 101 JCB earthmovers travelled together along the Super Corridor, creating a remarkable visual tribute to the workforce that builds our nation. The rally symbolised coordination, patriotism and the spirit of community contribution.

  • Highlights: 101-vehicle convoy, decorated machines and organised parade
  • Purpose: Celebrate Republic Day and honour earthmover operators
  • Audience: Open to the public — spectators should follow marshals and safety instructions
